The Intelligent Materials & Systems (IMS) Lab at Nanyang Technological University conducts cutting-edge research in spintronics, magnetic materials, and smart devices. Our work spans fundamental physics to applied device engineering, with a focus on next-generation memory, sensing, and computing technologies.

Our research areas include spin-orbit torque switching, magnetic tunnel junctions, domain wall dynamics, skyrmions, and neuromorphic devices. We work closely with industry partners and collaborate with leading research institutions worldwide.

The lab is led by Professor Wen Siang Lew and is part of the Division of Physics and Applied Physics within the School of Physical and Mathematical Sciences at NTU.
